BalanceFactor=Height(LeftSubtree)−Height(RightSubtree)

8.5 AVL Rebalancing

After every insertion:

Step 1

Insert normally like BST.

Step 2

Update heights.

Step 3

Calculate balance factor.

Step 4

Apply rotation if needed.


| Case | Rotation       |
| ---- | -------------- |
| LL   | Right Rotation |
| RR   | Left Rotation  |
| LR   | Left + Right   |
| RL   | Right + Left   |

Height Update Formula

Height=1+max(Height(Left),Height(Right))
